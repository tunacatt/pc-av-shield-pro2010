## PC Anti Virus Shield Pro 2010 - Features

![Activate Popup](https://i.ibb.co/fVQw9k20/Activation-Popup.png)
### *Activate or die*
If you do not activate, you will get an unclosable popup telling you to activate. And no, you won't hide it with another window. Don't worry, it is always on top, you can't minimize it, you can't `Alt`+`F4` it. 


![Scan reminder](https://i.ibb.co/6cYrHm3z/Scan-Remind.png) ![Threats Found](https://i.ibb.co/DH9Gdgz0/Threats-Found.png)  ![Activate Notification](https://i.ibb.co/v64tb38F/Activate.png)
### ~~Popups~~ Notifications
There are 3 types of notifications:

- **Scan reminder**
  
	A reminder to scan your PC, because if you will not scan your PC of course you will get virus'd
- **Threats found**
  
	If threats were found on your PC, you will get notified to remove them
- **Activation reminder**
  
	What else say, this one tells you to activate if you haven't done that yet

Of course, you can disable them (only if you activate)	

![Scan window](https://i.ibb.co/gZmP4342/Scan.png)
### Scans
Which antivirus doesn't have a scan feature? You can scan your PC and remove threats with this one!
